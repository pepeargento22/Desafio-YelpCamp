Latik Riverside $150/night

Latik Riverside is a campground located in the Philippines which consists of a river running along the valley. As it is surrounded by mountains, you can take up any of its many trails which provide a majestic view of the whole valley. There also are fishing hotspots on certain parts of the river for those interested.
